Mae Borowski, a college dropout, returns to her hometown of Possum Springs, a decrepit former mining community,
in an effort to pick up her aimless former life and make amends with the friends she left behind. But things have changed.
Her pals have changed and grown, and home seems different today. The wind is getting colder and the leaves are dropping.
As the light diminishes, strange things start to happen.

In addition, something is in the woods.
